# EDILAS: The Living Pilgrimage

**A dark survival RPG where every step forward is earned through blood, fire, and will.**

Journey through a corrupted world where seven biomes stand between you and the mythical paradise of EDILAS. Build colonies, forge alliances, master combat, and survive the pilgrimage -- or die trying.

### [>>> PLAY NOW <<<](https://stabrea.github.io/edilas-game/)

*Best experienced in Chrome or Firefox on desktop. Requires keyboard input.*

---

## The World

A sprawling open world corrupted by an ancient force. Seven distinct biomes, each guarded by a colossal Titan boss, form the path to EDILAS. The corruption spreads. The clock is ticking.

**Biomes:** Haven | Rotwood | Ashlands | Drowned Depths | Bone Wastes | The Void | EDILAS

## Features

**Combat & Classes**
- 6 playable classes -- Pilgrim, Warrior, Ranger, Mage, Shadow, Paladin -- each with unique skill trees and mastery progression
- Deep combat with combo chains, parry windows, dodge i-frames, and elemental damage
- 16 status effects including burn, poison, freeze, bleed, and corruption
- Weapon proficiency system that rewards specialization

**Enemies & Bosses**
- 51 enemy types with behavior-tree AI across all biomes
- 82 boss encounters including 7 Titan bosses with multi-phase state-machine AI
- Wildlife ecosystem with deer, boar, wolves, and fish

**Progression & Exploration**
- 62 quests spanning 7 story chapters with hand-crafted and procedural generation
- 304 items across weapons, armor, consumables, materials, and crafting recipes
- 8 professions with tiered mastery bonuses
- Corruption-gated biome progression with Titan boss gates
- Infinite procedural dungeons through the Rift system

**Colony Building**
- 12 buildable colony structures with upgrade tiers
- NPC recruitment and task assignment
- Tech tree research system
- 8 named NPCs with full dialogue, schedules, and relationship systems

**Survival Systems**
- Hunger, thirst, temperature, and stamina management
- Day/night cycle with dynamic weather across seasons
- Farming with 10 crop types and seasonal growth
- Fishing minigame with 15 fish species
- Cooking system with food buffs

**Living World**
- Dynamic weather (7 types) with biome-specific patterns
- Seasonal festivals and random world events
- Faction reputation system affecting NPC interactions
- In-game mail system with NPC messages and rewards
- 35+ achievements across 9 categories

**Endgame**
- New Game+ cycles with escalating difficulty
- Legendary hunts and endgame quests
- Shadow World dimension with alternate mechanics
- Procedural Rift dungeons with stacking modifiers

## Tech Highlights

Built from scratch as a browser-native game with no external game frameworks.

- Custom ECS (Entity-Component-System) architecture for high-performance entity management
- Dual rendering pipeline: WebGL 2.0 with Canvas2D fallback
- Procedural world generation with infinite chunk streaming
- A* pathfinding and behavior-tree AI for all entities
- State-machine driven boss AI with cinematic phase transitions
- Positional audio with dynamic music crossfading
- Accessibility features: colorblind modes, high contrast, font scaling, gamepad support
- Cloud saves and cross-device sync

## System Requirements

- Modern browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 90+, Edge 90+, Safari 15+)
- Keyboard required (gamepad supported)
- Recommended: dedicated GPU for WebGL rendering
- ~240MB initial download (sprite assets)
